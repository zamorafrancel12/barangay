About Barangay 181, Pangarap Village

Barangay 181 is strategically located in Caloocan City, making it easily accessible from various parts of Metro Manila. It is bordered by Barangay 180 to the north, Barangay 182 to the south, Barangay 178 to the east, and Barangay 183 to the west.

The barangay is conveniently connected to major roads and highways, providing residents with easy access to other parts of the city. Public transportation options, such as buses, jeepneys, and tricycles, are readily available, making commuting a breeze.

Hon. Bernardo Bisnar Quiboy

Barangay Captain

Bernardo Bisnar Quiboy has been serving as the Barangay Captain of Barangay 181, Pangarap Village, Caloocan City since the 2018 elections. His tenure has continued due to the postponement of the scheduled 2020 barangay and Sangguniang Kabataan elections, which were delayed to 2023.

As Barangay Captain, Quiboy is responsible for local governance and community leadership in Barangay 181, overseeing the implementation of barangay policies and the delivery of basic services. His role is crucial in local administration and community development within his jurisdiction.
